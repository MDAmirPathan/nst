About us and History

New Star Technologies is a privately owned international BPO and call center which primarily caters to the telecom and energy sectors in the United States. Founded in 2004 in Annandale, Virginia, the firm specializes in a variety of customer care management services. From its ultra-modern state-of-the-art facilities located across the globe, including Jaipur and Mumbai in India, the company operates both inbound and outbound processes.

Team

With a bright and experienced workforce of around 40 employees, New Star Technologies works around the clock to ensure complete client and customer satisfaction. The supremely efficient power-packed team, which is a balanced mix of young competent professionals and seasoned industry stalwarts, provides an array of innovative cost-effective services. Right from handling queries and managing sales, the employees of the company offer strategic solutions to enhance sales and maintain healthy relationships with customers.
